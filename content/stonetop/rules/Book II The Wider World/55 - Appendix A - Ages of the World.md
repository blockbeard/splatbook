# Ages of the World

Most events are listed in the order they occurred, but events in *italics* and starting with **?** are approximate, their specific placement unclear. Did construction begin on the Makers' Roads before or after the Tempest Lords' capital sank? Did Brennan become Marshedge's marshal before or after the Forest Folk disappeared? That's for you and your group to decide, if it matters.

## The Primordial Age

*Eons ago, over a span beyond reckoning.*

- Light fills the void; the **[[46 - The Things Below#The Things Below|Things Below]]** were already there
- **[[36 - Primordial Powers#**Questions**|First Creators]]** become aware of themselves
- **?** *[[36 - Primordial Powers#**Other primordial entities**|The first of the great spirits]]* *arise*
- Things Below stoke the Creators' egos, urge war
- First Creators forge the world and trap the Things Below inside
- **[[28 - The Labyrinth#**Thraulgwyn raider**|Tcaventes, Shackle and Key]]**, is set to guard the **[[28 - The Labyrinth#**Tcaventes, Shackle and Key**|Utterwell]]**
- **?** *The gods* (page [[20 - Gods and Religion#Gods and religion|178\)]] *do many legendary things*
- **?** *The earliest peoples evolve or are created*

## Time of the Makers

*Millennia ago, over a span of centuries (at least).*

- Rise of the **Tempest Lords**' civilization [[45 - Tempest Lords|Tempest Lords]]
- Tempest Lords establish their empire
- Rise of other **Maker** groups [[30 - The Makers|The Makers]]
- Tempest Lords found "Northernmost" (the **[[11 - The Dread River#**Winter**|Ruins at Road's End]]**)
- Tempest Lord homeland sinks, empire crumbles; other Makers grow in power
- **?** *Construction of the Makers' Roads begins* (page [[31 - The Makers' Roads#The Makers' Roads|270]])
- **?** *[[24 - Green Lords#Green Lords|Green Lords]]* *[[12 - Fae#Fae|breed the Fae]]*
- **?** *[[34 - North Manmarch#North Manmarch|Manmarchers]]* *[[11 - The Dread River#The Dread River|come from across the Dread River]]*
- **[[39 - The Ruined Tower#Stormcatcher's tower|Stormcatcher]]** establishes her Tower in the Flats
- Fae rebellion! the Green Lords call on the Things Below
- **[[43 - Stone Lords#Stone Lords|Stone Lords]]** destroy the heart of the Green Lord's territory, creating what is now **[[13 - Ferrier's Fen#Ferrier's Fen|Ferrier's Fen]]**
- Siege of **[[52 - Vor Svetelik#Vor Svetelik|Vor Svetelik]]**; Green Lords retreat, become **[[15 - Fomoraij#Names|Fomoraij]]**
- Stone Lords' servants start to plot rebellion and embrace the Things Below

Were the civilizations of the **[[29 - Lygos and the South#Lygos and the South|South]]** founded during the Time of the Makers, or even before? Or perhaps during the Time of the Barrow Builders?

## Time of Cataclysm

*Centuries ago, over a span of decades.*

- Stone Lords' servants rebel, unleash the Things Below, sink Dulenn and create **[[06 - Blackwater Lake#Blackwater Lake|Blackwater Lake]]**
- **?** *"Northernmost" overrun by horrors from the Dread River*
- Stone Lords hunted down by rebels
- Teirstered falls, becomes **[[47 - Three Coven Lake#Three Coven Lake|Three Coven Lake]]**
- Refugees start heading to Stormcatcher's tower
- **?** *[[54 - The Whitefang Mountains#**Shantyara**|Howling Curse]]* *[[38 - Rime Lords#Rime Lords|causes Rime Lord civilization to nearly collapse]]*
- **?** *Volcanic eruptions, Moratia Rubra destroyed, Forge Lord society collapses* [[18 - Forge Lords|Forge Lords]]
- More refugees arrive at Stormcatcher's tower
- Surviving Rime Lords sacrifice themselves to contain the Howling Curse
- Stormcatcher's tower falls

## Time of the Barrow Builders

*Centuries ago, over a span of generations.*

- First barrows built to honor freedom fighters
- **[[46 - The Things Below#**Thrall**|Emanations]]** of the Things Below roam freely, are worshipped/appeased
- Warlords/sorcerer-kings vie for power, build more elaborate barrows
- Battles fought over Three Coven Lake, the Ruins at Road's End, etc.
- Refugees, enslavement, human sacrifice, all-around bad times
- Subjects of the Barrow Builders overthrow/abandon/imprison their tyrannical rulers
- Certain bands of Barrow Builders flee into the **[[28 - The Labyrinth#The Labyrinth|Labyrinth]]**
