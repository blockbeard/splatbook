# The Crombil

A great scaly wurm, hundreds of feet long. It sleeps somewhere beneath the **[[23 - The Great Wood#The Great Wood|Great Wood]]**, waking every few decades to chew and burn a swath of destruction.

## **What is it, exactly?**

That's for you to decide. Maybe…

- … it's a primordial **[[36 - Primordial Powers#**Other primordial entities**|titan]]**, a remnant of the first age;
- … the **[[24 - Green Lords#Names|Green Lords]]** made it, as a weapon, or just because they could;
- … it's the first and oldest **[[18 - Forge Lords#**Draventao**|draventao]]**, a mighty Forge Lord who succumbed to the fire within;
- … it's a manifestation of the **[[46 - The Things Below#The Things Below|Things Below]]**, all gluttony and fire and nightmare; or
- … it's just a big, dumb beast.

## **Hooks**

- The PCs wish to destroy some fell, in destructible artifact, and learn that feeding it to the Crombil might work.
- The Crombil will soon wake, and an important site—like a ruin of the Green Lords or a **[[37 - Red Groves#Red Groves|Red Grove]]**—lies in its path. The PCs must act soon to explore or preserve the site before it's destroyed.
- It's been almost fifty years since the Crombil woke, and its last rampage ended maybe a day or two from Stonetop. Elders remember the smoke and the sounds of destruction, but few ever knew the cause. But now the Crombil stirs, and this time it might head towards Stonetop.
- The Crombil's periodic rampages have been drawing it towards **[[52 - Vor Svetelik#Vor Svetelik|Vor Svetelik]]**. This next time, it will plow into the ruined city and release any number of terrors from stasis and imprisonment, perhaps even the **[[15 - Fomoraij#Names|Fomoraij]]** themselves. ## **Signs & portents**

- Tremors and earthquakes, of increasing frequency and intensity
- Dreams of shifting earth, hunger, fire, and doom
- A small hill, covered in trees and brush but strangely warm and venting smoke
- Beasts and spirits giving wider and wider berth to the Crombil's lair
- Strangers arrive in town and talk of "the Awakening"
- Dreamers wake from troubling dreams, "the Crombil" on their lips
- Some suggest offering sacrifice to appease the Crombil, others feel com pelled to go and worship as it wakes
- Vast flocks of birds fleeing to the west
- A terrible grinding and cracking of earth and trees; a pillar of smoke; a deep and bellowing cry

## **The Crombil, Awakened**

*Solitary*, *huge*, *terrifying*, *fearless*, *fireproof* **HP** 26; **Armor** 3 (scales, boney ridges)

**Damage** jaws like a cave d12+7 (*reach*, *forceful*, *messy*, 3 piercing), thrashing coils d12+5 (*close*, *area*, *forceful*)

**Special qualities** fiery heat, stomach like a furnace, too big to hurt with most weapons

## **Instinct** to devour

- ä Chomp right through anything
- ä Swallow up someone whole
- ä Upend the terrain around it
- ä Belch cinders, start fires

When someone's been swallowed by the Crombil, start using these moves against them:

- ä Batter them with debris, cinders (d6 damage)
- ä Make them fight for any sort of purchase or bearings
- ä Shove them further towards its furnace-like stomach
- ä Blister skin, burn lungs, singe hair
