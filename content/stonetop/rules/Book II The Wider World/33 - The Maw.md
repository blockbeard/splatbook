# The Maw

There's a cave in the **[[23 - The Great Wood#The Great Wood|Great Wood]]**, a few hours north of Stonetop. No underbrush grows within 30 feet of it, and bones litter the entrance. Trappers and hunters avoid it, and claim to have heard strange noises coming from its direction especially at night.

### **Impressions**

- An unnatural quiet, the forest holding its breath
- A blighted patch, spreading out from the cave, where only grey lichen grows
- Bones strewn about, broken & chewed
- The black void of the cave mouth
- Those strange noises from within

Tip: speak softly, hold very still. Pause a little longer between words, sentences.

## **Questions**

- Who among you have been here before? What brought you?
- What do the noises from the Maw (supposedly) sound like?
- What do you think is in there? Why?

## **Hooks**

- A beloved animal goes missing; someone saw it near the Maw.
- A hunter reports strange figures (or tracks) coming and going.
- A sickness afflicts the town; the treatment is an herb that grows near the Maw, but that must be harvested under moonlight.

### **What could be in there?**

That's for you to decide, but perhaps it's...

- … a **[[23 - The Great Wood#**Instinct** to lure prey|hagr]]**, afflicted with some withering curse;
- … a brutal, gluttonous **[[12 - Fae#Fae|Fae]]**, and an entrance to its **[[12 - Fae#**Getting there**|domain]]**;
- … an idol or emanation of the **[[46 - The Things Below#The Things Below|Things Below]]**;
- … a passage to one of the **Fomoraij**'s [[15 - Fomoraij#Names|Names]] twisted realms;
- … a **[[10 - Death and the Undying#**Pyped**|specter]]** or a pack of **[[10 - Death and the Undying#**Pyped**|wraiths]]**, terrible and ancient and only partly bound; or
- … something else entirely.
